# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People using a WeChat Mini Program to photograph storage containers, verify recognized items, and find or manage those items later. They are usually in a quick household workflow: take a photo, correct obvious recognition mistakes, save the container, and rely on reminders for items that can expire.

## Product Purpose

Find Things helps users turn container photos into searchable item records. Success means the recognition review, item editing, reminder setup, and save flow stay fast enough that users can finish while they are physically organizing things.

## Brand Personality

Warm, practical, and lightly playful through the Xiao Lan voice. The interface should feel helpful and calm, not technical or fussy.

## Anti-references

Avoid heavy dashboard styling, decorative marketing layouts, repeated permission prompts, crowded controls, and flows that ask the user to confirm the same intent twice.

## Design Principles

- Keep photo-to-save workflows short and forgiving.
- Treat WeChat permissions as a rare interruption, not a repeated step.
- Preserve in-app fallbacks whenever external messaging cannot be guaranteed.
- Favor stable, compact layouts that survive long recognition text and small phone widths.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Controls should remain readable on small WeChat device viewports, avoid horizontal scrolling, keep tap targets comfortable, and avoid relying on color alone for state.
